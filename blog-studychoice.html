My Study Choice

I researched study programs that focus on programming in my home country of the United States as well as across Europe. I really like the HZ ICT program.
I chose this study program because it is a multi-faceted curriculum and degree. It fits well with my passion for all things technology, specifically game creation and design. Additionally, I am strongly interested in programming and the many applications in which it is used to run our world everyday. Due to my previous experience with learning Lua, I've understood more about why I enjoy programming and tech.
After graduating, I plan to use the technical and soft skills I learn during the program to build a tech company from the ground up. I anticipate creating a company from its inception will be exciting, rewarding, and a great deal of work. I expect to develop the necessary technical and crucial team-building skills during the program.
