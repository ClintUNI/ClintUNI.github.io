How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Head to the linked HZ Print Page > log into your account > upload the files you wish to print > set additional settings, if nescessary > confirm your print request > head to the the printers at HZ, located on each floor > scan your student ID card > choose your printing options > finished.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Free parking is possible on the car park of PZEM at the Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg. Reveal your HZ pass at the barrier > enter the parking lot > park the car.
