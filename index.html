My ICT Journey

A little about why the ICT field suits me.

Logic is my Strength

Programming, and computer science in general, is commonly refered to as a tool(s) to solve logic based problems. Logic was always one of my strong suits and so I believe learning to use a tool to help solve logic problems could be considered a rather smart decision.

Learning gains Power

Learning, and knowledge itself, is the root of power. By learning to program and handle related projects it gives me the ability to make a living, solve my own issues for free, help friends and family and have a future career in an ever expanding career-path.
